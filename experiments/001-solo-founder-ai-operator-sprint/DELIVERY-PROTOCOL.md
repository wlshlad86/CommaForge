# Manual Delivery Protocol

## Before delivery

- Confirm payment through Commas.
- Define the single workstream in scope.
- Agree what is excluded and what requires founder approval.
- Avoid collecting credentials or unnecessary personal data.

## Sprint

1. Inventory existing sources and tools.
2. Identify the governing objective.
3. Separate facts, decisions, assumptions, and unknowns.
4. Map current state, actors, dependencies, and next actions.
5. Create the canonical seed.
6. Create decision and open-loop registers.
7. Define permissions, refusals, and escalation points.
8. Test a clean handoff against a real next action.

## Completion evidence

- Founder can identify the source of key claims.
- Operator can state current objective and next action.
- Unresolved questions are visible.
- No consequential action is implied without authority.
- Founder accepts the handoff as useful.
