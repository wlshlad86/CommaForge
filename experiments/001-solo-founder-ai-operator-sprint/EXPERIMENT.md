# Experiment 001 — Solo Founder AI Operator Sprint

## Hypothesis

Four solo founders will each pay £250 for guided implementation of a structured AI operator around one real workstream.

## Customer

Solo founders with active work, fragmented project context, repeated AI handoff friction, or stalled execution caused by poor operational continuity.

## Offer

One guided sprint that maps and seeds a single live workstream into a trustworthy operator-ready structure.

## Deliverables

- workstream map;
- canonical project seed;
- decision log;
- open-loop register;
- boundaries and escalation rules;
- final handoff and next-action plan.

## Price and target

- Price: £250 paid through Commas
- Customers: 4
- Revenue target: £1,000 gross received
- Outreach sample: 20 founders

## Protocol

1. Publish the minimum viable offer on Commas.
2. Identify 20 suitable founders.
3. Send personal, non-automated outreach.
4. Record responses and objections.
5. Take payment before delivery.
6. Deliver manually.
7. Record time, friction, customer outcome, and feedback.
8. Decide whether to continue, change, or stop.

## Decision rule

- **Continue:** at least one paid customer and delivery creates a credible useful outcome.
- **Revise:** strong conversations or intent but no payment; identify the weakest element of customer/problem/promise/proof/price/channel.
- **Stop:** no payment and no strong problem evidence after 20 qualified approaches, or delivery cannot be made safe and useful at the price.
