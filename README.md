# CommaForge

CommaForge is a commercial intelligence and experimentation system for earning Richie's first **£1,000 in gross customer sales actually received through Commas**.

The repository exists to turn evidence into offers, paid experiments, delivery learning, and better commercial decisions. It is not a speculative software architecture project.

## Current milestone

### Experiment 001 — Solo Founder AI Operator Sprint

- Offer: guided implementation of one real founder workstream with a structured AI operator
- Price: £250
- Target: four paying customers
- Validation: publish a minimal Commas offer, approach 20 founders, take payment before delivery, and deliver manually
- Revenue milestone: £250 × 4 = £1,000 gross sales received through Commas

## Start here

1. Read [CANONICAL-STATE.md](CANONICAL-STATE.md).
2. Execute [Experiment 001](experiments/001-solo-founder-ai-operator-sprint/EXPERIMENT.md).
3. Update the [scoreboard](scoreboard/SCOREBOARD.md) after every meaningful action.
4. Record new facts in the [source register](intelligence/SOURCE-REGISTER.md).
5. Record decisions in `decisions/` before changing scope.

## Evidence labels

- **FACT** — directly supported by an authoritative user decision or cited primary source
- **INFERENCE** — reasoned from facts but not directly established
- **HYPOTHESIS** — a testable proposition
- **UNKNOWN** — material information not yet established

## Scope boundary

Palantir-style mapping, GBrain, knowledge graphs, embeddings, multi-agent systems, Swallow, and other surfaces may become useful later. They must not displace the current commercial loop unless evidence shows they are required to make or deliver a sale.
