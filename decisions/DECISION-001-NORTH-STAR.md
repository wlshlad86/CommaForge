# DECISION-001 — Revenue North Star

**Date:** 2026-07-18  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Authority:** Richie

## Decision

CommaForge's first milestone is £1,000 in gross customer sales actually received through Commas.

## Reason

This measures real market behaviour with minimal accounting friction. Net profit, refund-adjusted revenue, pipeline, attention, and technical completion answer different questions.

## Consequence

Every Phase 1 task must either enable a sale, deliver a paid promise, measure the experiment, or preserve evidence required for those actions.
