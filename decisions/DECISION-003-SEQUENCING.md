# DECISION-003 — Commercial Before Architectural Expansion

**Date:** 2026-07-18  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Authority:** Richie, clarified through the revenue-first decision

## Decision

The revenue experiment is primary. FanBasis → Commas mapping remains useful supporting intelligence. The earlier ambitious five-layer, Palantir-style, GBrain, embeddings, and multi-agent concepts are not Phase 1 implementation commitments.

## Reconsider when

A specific repeated sales or delivery bottleneck has evidence showing that one of those capabilities is the smallest effective solution.
