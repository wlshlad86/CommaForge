# FanBasis → Commas Transition Notes

**Status:** Partial primary-source mapping  
**Retrieved:** 2026-07-18

## Verified

- The current Commas homepage explicitly announces that FanBasis is now Commas.
- The support estate remains hosted at `support.fanbasis.com` but is branded as the Commas Knowledge Base.
- Some help article titles still use FanBasis terminology.
- The current Commas terms state that FanBasis, Inc. does business under both FanBasis and Commas and treats both branded surfaces as one platform.
- `fanbasis.com` still exposes active product, portal, legal, and historical campaign surfaces.

## Inference

The transition is not a clean domain replacement. It is a layered evolution in which legal entity, support infrastructure, old content, product surfaces, and current brand coexist.

## Unknown

- Exact public rebrand date and transition sequence
- Which legacy endpoints remain operational dependencies
- Whether seller accounts and data migrated without structural changes
- Whether Commas introduces a new product model or primarily a brand/product expansion
