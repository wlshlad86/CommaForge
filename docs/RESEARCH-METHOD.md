# Research Method

## Purpose

Research exists to improve a commercial decision or experiment. It is not a substitute for contacting customers.

## Capture protocol

For each source, record:

- source owner;
- exact URL or conversation title;
- retrieval date;
- evidence type;
- concise factual extraction;
- unresolved uncertainty;
- decision or experiment affected.

## Evidence priority

1. Richie's newest explicit project decision
2. Direct customer behaviour and payment data
3. Current primary Commas/FanBasis sources
4. Recovered project chats and uploaded artifacts
5. Secondary sources
6. Inference and hypothesis

## Conflict protocol

When evidence conflicts:

1. retain both claims;
2. mark the conflict;
3. identify authority and date;
4. prefer the newest authoritative decision for current action;
5. preserve the superseded item in `archive/`.
