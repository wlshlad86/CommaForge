# Phase 1 — First £1,000 Through Commas

## Objective

Earn £1,000 in gross customer sales actually received through Commas.

## Included

1. Create the Solo Founder AI Operator Sprint as a minimal Commas offer.
2. Produce only the sales material needed to explain and purchase it.
3. Approach 20 relevant solo founders directly.
4. Take payment before starting delivery.
5. Deliver each sprint manually using the defined delivery protocol.
6. Capture objections, time spent, outcomes, and customer feedback.
7. Improve the offer only from observed evidence.

## Excluded

- building a general-purpose knowledge graph;
- embeddings or vector infrastructure;
- a multi-agent company;
- autonomous outreach;
- a full software product;
- using Yash Daftary's attention as the success criterion;
- exhaustive FanBasis/Commas archaeology that delays the offer.

## Exit criteria

Phase 1 completes when cumulative gross customer sales received through Commas reach £1,000.

## Failure signal

If 20 suitable founders receive a clear offer and none pays, stop and review the target customer, problem, promise, proof, price, and channel before increasing outreach volume.
