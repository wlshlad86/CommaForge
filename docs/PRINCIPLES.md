# Operating Principles

1. **Revenue is the milestone.** Attention and activity are supporting signals, not the outcome.
2. **Payment is stronger evidence than praise.** Validate willingness to pay before delivery.
3. **Manual before automated.** Learn the workflow through real delivery before building systems around guesses.
4. **Preserve provenance.** Every durable claim should retain its source and retrieval date.
5. **Separate epistemic status.** Facts, inferences, hypotheses, and unknowns must not blur together.
6. **One brain, many surfaces—later.** Swallow and future interfaces can share intelligence, but Phase 1 stays commercially narrow.
7. **Architecture follows evidence.** Add technical structure only when a measured constraint requires it.
8. **Inspectability over theatre.** Decisions and results must be open to challenge from the evidence.
